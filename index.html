Our Story

About AutoLux

Founded in 2010, AutoLux has grown from a small local agency to one of the most trusted premium car rental brands in the United States. Our mission is to make luxury accessible and every drive unforgettable.

We maintain a fleet of over 250 meticulously serviced vehicles, ranging from economy compacts to ultra-luxury supercars, all equipped with the latest safety technology and connectivity features.
